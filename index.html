NORTHERN CUP CAFÉ

We take great pride in sourcing only the finest and freshest coffee beans from around the world, which we roast in-house to ensure the highest quality and taste. Our expert baristas are trained to craft each beverage to perfection, using traditional techniques and modern brewing methods.

About us

Our baristas are passionate about their craft and take great care in preparing each and every cup. We use only the highest quality beans, roasted to perfection, to ensure that every sip is rich, smooth, and delicious. But coffee is just the beginning at Northern Cup. We offer a wide variety of specialty drinks, including lattes, cappuccinos, and mochas, all made with the same attention to detail and commitment to quality that we bring to our coffee. And if you're looking for a bite to eat, we've got you covered there, too. Our menu features a range of delicious breakfast and lunch items, from classic bagels and cream cheese to hearty sandwiches and wraps. So whether you're looking for a quick pick-me-up on your way to work or a leisurely lunch with friends, Northern Cup is the perfect spot. Come on in and join us for a cup today!
